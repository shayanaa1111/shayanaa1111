## 🎯 About Me

I'm an **AI/ML engineer and entrepreneur** on a mission to build transformative intelligent systems. As a **Computer Science graduate** from The Shaikh Ayaz University, I specialize in **machine learning**, **deep learning**, and **emerging AI technologies** like RAG systems and AI agents.

I founded **DYLP** (Digital Youth Leadership Program) to empower young professionals across Pakistan, and co-created **SindhiMate**, an innovative AI-powered platform for Sindhi language preservation. My work focuses on creating **AI solutions for real-world challenges** in education, healthcare, and sustainability.

**🏆 Achievements Highlight:**

- Sindhi Poetry & Story Generation with AI

- **Tech Stack:** FastAPI, Python, SQLite, Docker, Gemini API, SMTP
- **Features:** 50+ simultaneous users, real-time matching
